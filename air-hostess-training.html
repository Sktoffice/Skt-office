Air Hostess Training

Your Gateway to a Glamorous Aviation Career

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum Qualification: 10+2 or equivalent
  • Age: 18 to 27 years
  • Height & Appearance: 155+ cm, good grooming

Skills Required

  • Fluent Communication
  • Customer Service & Problem Solving
  • Emergency Handling

Training Details

  • Theory: Aviation, Safety, First Aid
  • Practical: In-flight service, Grooming, Mock Drills

Career Opportunities

  • Cabin Crew for Domestic & International Airlines
  • Ground Staff & Airline Customer Service
  • Hospitality & Tourism Sector

FAQs

Q1. Do I need a degree to become an air hostess?

No, 10+2 is usually sufficient. However, a diploma in aviation is helpful.

Q2. Is training mandatory?

Yes, airlines prefer professionally trained candidates.

Q3. What is the average course duration?

Typically 6 months to 1 year depending on the institute.
